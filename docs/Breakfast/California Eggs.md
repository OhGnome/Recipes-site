# California Eggs

## Ingredients
- Eggs
- Crispy Onions
- Tabasco
- Salt/Pepper

## Method
1. Fry eggs
2. As the eggs cook, sprinkle on Crispy Onions and Tabasco
3. Add Salt/Pepper to taste
